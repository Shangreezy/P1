Culture & Heritage

Zimbabwe’s culture is a rich blend of ancient traditions, colonial history, and modern influences. Visitors can experience vibrant art, traditional ceremonies, and the famous Zimbabwean hospitality.

Traditional Highlights

  • Mbira Music: The thumb piano is a national symbol of spiritual connection and storytelling.
  • Stone Sculptures: Shona sculptors are world-renowned for their expressive stone artwork.
  • Festivals: Events such as the Harare International Festival of the Arts (HIFA) celebrate music, dance, and creativity.

Visitors can also enjoy Zimbabwe’s delicious traditional cuisine, including sadza, roasted meat, and local vegetables served with peanut sauce.
