Top Tourist Destinations in Zimbabwe

Victoria Falls

One of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World, known locally as “The Smoke That Thunders.”

Hwange National Park

Zimbabwe’s largest national park, home to elephants, lions, and diverse bird species.

Great Zimbabwe Ruins

Ancient stone city ruins showcasing Zimbabwe’s historical civilization and heritage.

Lake Kariba

A massive artificial lake offering boat cruises, fishing, and scenic sunsets.

Matobo Hills

Granite rock formations and sacred caves decorated with ancient rock art.
