Wildlife & Adventure

Zimbabwe is home to diverse ecosystems, offering unforgettable wildlife encounters and adventure experiences. The country’s national parks are among Africa’s best-kept secrets for safaris.

Top Safari Parks

  • Hwange National Park: The largest in Zimbabwe, home to over 100 mammal species including elephants and lions.
  • Mana Pools: UNESCO World Heritage Site known for canoe safaris along the Zambezi River.
  • Matobo National Park: Famous for its rhino population and granite rock formations.

Adventure lovers can also enjoy bungee jumping, white-water rafting, and scenic helicopter flights over Victoria Falls.
