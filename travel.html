Travel Information

Planning your trip to Zimbabwe is easy with the right information. Whether you’re traveling for adventure, culture, or relaxation, the country offers warm hospitality and convenient travel options.

Before You Go

  • Visas: Available online (eVisa) or on arrival for most nationalities.
  • Best Time to Visit: May to October — cooler and ideal for safaris.
  • Transport: Domestic flights, intercity buses, and guided tours available from major cities.
  • Currency: Both US Dollar (USD) and Zimbabwean Dollar (ZWG) are commonly used.

Remember to respect local customs, support sustainable tourism, and enjoy Zimbabwe’s warm, friendly atmosphere.
